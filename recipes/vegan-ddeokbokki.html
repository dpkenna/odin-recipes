Vegan Ddeokbokki

Description

A vegan version of the classic Korean street food. Spicy and sweet with delicious chewy rice cakes. Hold the fish cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of cylinder shaped rice cake (ddeok)
  • 4 cups of water
  • 6x8 inch dried kelp
  • 1/3 cup hot pepper paste (gochujang)
  • 1 tablespoon hot pepper flakes (gochugaru)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 3 green onions, cut into 3-inch long pieces

Steps

  1. Add the water and dried kelp to a shallow pot or pan.
  2. Boil for 15 minutes over medium high heat without the lid.
  3. Combine hot pepper paste, hot pepper flakes, and sugar in a small bowl.
  4. Remove the kelp from the pot and add the rice cakes, the mixture in the bowl, and the green onion. The stock will be about 2 1/2 cups.
  5. Stir gently with a wooden spoon when it starts to boil. Keep stirring until the rice cake turns soft and the sauce thickens and looks shiny.
  6. Remove from the heat and serve hot.
